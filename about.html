A little about me

My Story

Who I am?

I love design and film in all its many shapes and sizes. I love creative collaboration and I love to support people, places and organizations that do good. I’m always eager to learn new skills or develop existing ones especially when it helps build something great. I’m avidly curious and have followed that curiosity in creative work, community organizing, technology nerdiness, travel… and much much more.

Where are you based?

I grew up on the outskirts of Paris in Les Ulis, France. I moved to Boulder Colorado for high school and college and have been exploring ever since. I currently live in Longmont Colorado with my partner and my little blue bird.

What is my professional experience?

I attended the University of Colorado for my Bachelors in Film Production and Environmental Design. I graduated with two bachelors in 2010 and went on to work in community development in Illinois and Oregon as a researcher and planner in sustainability and park development.

I received my Masters from Parsons, the New School for Design in 2015 in Design and Urban Ecologies and eventually found my way back to Colorado. I currently work for the University of Colorado’s Central Advancement as a designer and videographer assisting the fundraising process throughout the four campuses. In this position I pursued a Professional Certificate at the CU Boulder Museum and Field studies program.
